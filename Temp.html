Title description, April 1, 2020

Fair Enough to Slowdown

A wreaking havoc on the corporeal and economy due to side effects of recent pandemic has a dawdling effect on our lives and brought it to a standstill. Before it, our lives were busy, with chasing money, academics, company targets, false dreams and above all finding happiness in a materialistic world. With the fourth industrial revolution, we were busy buying stuff, clothes, gadgets and what not and carrying it as our esteemed possessions. Was it required, was it necessary? The roads are scarred, beaches are empty, our world has transformed into a pause. With the birds chirping and humans caged, whilst borders are not stopping nature and wildlife, humans have stepped into their shells. Can we live without it all? As the whole world is clogged, the answer may be YES or a definitely YES. It has affected all of us within depths of abyss. Adapt from nature, the way to give, the way to live, the way to expect less, the way to care, the way to nurture ourselves and others, Humanize and respect everyone, Forgive and forget, Above all live and let live. Life may become normal again and mankind may start revolving in their lives, yet the scars will remain imprinted upon us within our souls. However, let go of the past and focus on the present, for contributing to a better future.
